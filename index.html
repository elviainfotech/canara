Update Your PAN Card Details

Keeping your PAN (Permanent Account Number) card information up to date is crucial for smooth banking operations and compliance with regulatory requirements. Follow these simple steps to update your PAN card details with Canara Bank.

How to Update Your PAN Card:

1 Log in to your Canara Bank Net Banking account
2 Navigate to the 'Update Profile' or 'My Account' section
3 Select 'Update PAN Card Details'
4 Enter your PAN card number and other required information
5 Upload a scanned copy of your PAN card for verification
6 Submit the form and wait for confirmation

Did you know? You can also update your PAN card details through our mobile banking app. Download our official app for a seamless banking experience on the go.
